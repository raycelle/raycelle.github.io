PALMS Apps

Learning Experience Designer: Raycelle

A series of apps that are designed to promote active learning, as part of the Pedagogic Active Learning Mobile Solutions Project (PALMS) at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

I supported the learning experience design of 3 PALMS apps: YoTeach, Badaboom and Cell Game, including the following responsibilities:

  • Worked with UI designer and developers on the design, implementation and testing of new features on four internally developed web applications for learning
  • Gathered, responded to and raised user feedback
  • Prepared instructional materials for teachers and students
  • Supported the implementation, data collection and manuscript write up of testing and piloting sessions
