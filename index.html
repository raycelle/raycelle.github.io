I am enthusiastic team-player passionate about the design of engaging, effective and innovative learning experiences!

I recently completed my MS in Learning Design and Technology at the Stanford Graduate School of Education. My studies and work experiences at the Stanford Center to Support Excellence in Teaching (CSET) and the Hasso Plattner Institute of Design (d.school) has prepared me for the design, implementation and evaluation of technology-enhanced learning experiences that are strongly grounded in human-centered design and the learning sciences.

Prior to my studies at Stanford, I was a Project Associate in the PALMS project at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University and Lead Instructor at the educational startup, First Code Academy.
